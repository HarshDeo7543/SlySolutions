Frequently Asked Questions
What is the duration of Internship?
All Our Internships are for a period of 1 month only
Can I complete multiple tracks together?
We offer one internship track per month per student,ensuring in-depth learning and quality outcomes
Is there any fees for the internship?
The internship is free, but a ₹200 fee is required for project hosting to cover processing charges.
When will I receive the Offer Letter?
The offer letter is usually sent within a week after the completion of the selection process.
How to submit Tasks?
A Submission Form will be emailed 10-15 days after your Offer Letter
What is the criteria for selection?
Determination to learn and grow is all what is needed
When does the internship start?
Each batch starts at the start or mid of the month and ends on start or mid of next month respectively.
Is the internship Offline or Online?
The internship is primarily online, allowing participants to work from their location.
CIN is showing Invalid
If the CIN is showing invalid, please double-check the number you entered or contact support for assistance.
When will I get the Certificate?
The certificate will be issued within two weeks of completing all internship requirements.
